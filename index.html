ABOUT ME

Researcher, Writer, Drone Pilot

My goal is to help develop remote sensing and GIS tools to manage wildlife populations in a more efficient, safer, and less invasive way. Currently I manage the ASPIRE Center at the University of New Mexico, where I am also a PhD student in Geography and Environmental Studies. In my spare time, I love to explore the beautiful landscapes of New Mexico through running, backpacking, snowshoeing, and cross country skiing.
